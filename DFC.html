THE DFC CODE

MATCH DURATION

Matches are contested in a Best of 8 Rounds format. The first fighter to reach 5 round wins is declared the winner of the match. Each round has a strict 1 minute and 30 second limit.

DAMAGE JUDGING

If the 90-second timer expires without a Knockout, the designated DFC Referee will determine the winner based on Health Percentage, Armor Integrity, and Effective Striking. The Referee's decision is final.

RELEGATION POLICY

The 11th fighter (weekly qualifier winner) acts as the disruptor. If the newcomer defeats Rank #10, the newcomer takes that roster spot. The defeated pro is blacklisted and must win a new qualifier to return.

FINANCE & PAYOUTS

The Destiny Taxi Company takes a 15% commission on all house bets. The remaining purse is split between the fighter's salary and the Season Prize Pool. Rank #1 earns the Champion's Dividend at the end of Week 3.

THE CHAMPION'S SHIELD

The Rank #1 Champion is exempt from relegation but MUST defend the title every Friday night against the winner of the #2 vs #3 eliminator. Failure to appear results in immediate title vacancy.
